About This Model

Purpose: This predictive model identifies DC properties with high likelihood of being listed for sale based on financial distress indicators and ownership patterns.

Key Factors Analyzed:

  • Financial Distress: Properties with outstanding tax debt, liens, or high debt-to-assessment ratios
  • Ownership Patterns: Estate properties, trust ownership, or corporate entities that may indicate distressed situations
  • Property History: Time since last sale and assessment trends
  • Location Analysis: Ward-based market conditions and property types

Risk Categories:

  • Extremely High (80%+): Properties showing multiple distress indicators
  • Very High (60-80%): Properties with significant financial or ownership complexity
  • High (40-60%): Properties with moderate distress signals
  • Moderate (20-40%): Properties with some risk factors
  • Low (<20%): Properties with minimal distress indicators

Data Sources:

This model uses publicly available DC property records, tax data, and ownership information to generate predictions. All data is current as of the last model update.

Disclaimer: This is a predictive model for marketing purposes. Individual property situations may vary, and predictions should be used as guidance only.
